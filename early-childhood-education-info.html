Children Thrive with MUSE Early Childhood Education

Children's ideas - their passions and interests - shape the curriculum in the MUSE Early Childhood Education program. Teacher observations combined with student input create the basis for our unique approach to education that is a child-centric alternative to Montessori and Waldorf methods.

The MUSE School Difference

  • Personalized Learning:

    MUSE ECE teachers are trained to customize their approach to instruction, communication and classroom environment so each child can thrive.

  • Hands-on Learning:

    MUSE students learn through play, exploration and discovery on our 22-acre campus in the Santa Monica Mountains.

  • Emotional Intelligence:

    MUSE believes that social and emotional education is key to lifelong success. MUSE helps students be Open to new experiences, Resourceful when faced with challenges, and Persistent in their educational journey

  • Sustainable Living:

    From composting, to green gardening, to our goal of creating a campus with zero net emissions, every part of daily life at MUSE is focused on living in a sustainable way.
