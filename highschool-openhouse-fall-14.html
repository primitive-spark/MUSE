The MUSE School Difference

  • Personalized Learning:

    MUSE customizes each student's curriculum based on personal passions to maximize interest, comprehension and motivation.

  • Powerful Academics:

    MUSE students are assessed individually and encouraged to go beyond the classroom with real-world mentors.

  • Emotional Intelligence:

    MUSE's nurturing environment cultivates student self-efficacy, the belief in one's own ability to succeed, plus groundbreaking and highly effective communication methods.

  • Sustainable Living:

    MUSE students enjoy a unique seed-to-table lunch experience prepared in MUSE's on-site kitchen. Our students learn, hands-on, to be stewards of the earth's food, energy and water resources.
