See Why Students Thrive at MUSE Middle School

The MUSE school private education experience provides 6th - 8th grade students the opportunity to stay engaged and motivated every day. At MUSE, students won't just get a typical junior high school education. We combine a powerful academic curriculum with advanced teaching methods that capitalize on the student's interests.

It's the student's ideas - their passions and interests - that shape the curriculum. In addition, at MUSE, students learn to live a healthy lifestyle, to live peacefully with one another, and to live sustainably on Earth.

The MUSE School Difference

  • Personalized Learning:

    MUSE customizes each student's curriculum based on personal passions to maximize interest, comprehension and motivation.

  • Applied Academics:

    MUSE students engage in a demanding interdisciplinary curriculum that encourages them to go beyond the classroom into the community and work with real-world mentors.

  • Emotional Intelligence:

    MUSE cultivates student self-efficacy, the belief in one's own ability to succeed. Our nurturing environment helps students be Open to new experiences, Resourceful when faced with challenges, and Persistent in their educational journey.

  • Sustainable Living:

    Eco-literacy is an important part of our everyday educational experience. Our students learn, hands-on, to be stewards of the Earth's food, energy and water resources. From composting, to green gardening, to our goal of creating a campus with zero net emissions, every part of daily life at MUSE is focused on living in a sustainable way.
